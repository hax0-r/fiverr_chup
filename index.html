In a perfect world, customer makes a booking at 10.00 AM and the service provider will attend the customer at 10.00 AM slot. Or you join a queue and given an accurate wait times and be served accordingly. But this is NOT A PERFECT WORLD. Both service provider and the customers are never punctual - rendering the booking and estimated wait times useless. Why, despite appointment management, do people still wait for hours? They book at 10:00 AM but end up being attended to at 2:00PM?

Chup functions as a dynamic traffic manager, akin to police traffic at busy intersections during rush hour, handling inputs from both users/customers and service providers. Whether the information is provided by button inputs or gathered through automatic algorithm logics, Chup takes charge of processing it. Once detected, Chup promptly notifies both the user and service provider, and accordingly adjusts the ongoing live queue and future queue appointments.


The result? Pre-arrival customers receive notifications, enabling them to plan their arrival and minimize their wait times.

Post-arrival customers? They can plan to go or wait somewhere else. Research showed that not knowing how long to wait kills more than the wait itself. As for service providers, they can operate efficiently, stress-free, and guilt-free with Chup's effective crowd congestion management.

The dynamic situations mentioned here represent the main scenarios, but there are also numerous sub-situations under each of them. This list is not exhaustive. The logic and algorithm to handle these situations have been developed over the course of a few years. Apart from testing forecasted situations during development, Chup has undergone manual and automated testing after its development. Additionally, Chup has been trained on eight months' worth of real-world data and will continue to improve with more input and data over time.

Why works

Why Chup may solve your queue problems compared to other solutions on the market.

Dynamic Queue System

Using a machine learning algorithm, the app will work around the serving time, emergency breaks, delays, and all situations happening on the premises to ensure customers get updated Estimated Waiting Times (Live Queue) or new re-appointment times (Appointment Bookings).

No App to Download

There is no app to download for your users or your premises, as everything will be on the cloud. For existing customers, just upload your data, and they do not have to do anything, as the system will send notifications once the time comes.

Self-booking, Self-queue, and Self-cancellation

Your customers are able to do everything by themselves and avoid lengthy phone calls with receptionists to book, join the live queue, reschedule or cancel, and inform of any delays or arrivals.

Automatic frequent Reminders

The app will remind your queue positions for the live queue. For appointments, the app will send notifications by default—1 day, 4 hours, 2 hours, and 30 minutes prior—so they can plan and don’t forget.

Walk-in merge with Appointments

Most appointment and queue apps fail because, in reality, there are both walk-ins and appointments. Chup works by merging these two complex situations flawlessly.

No hardware to purchase

Chup works on the cloud. You do not have to purchase any hardware if you choose not to. You can even run your premises 100% using a mobile phone, as long as there is an internet connection at your premises. TVs and tablets are optional.

Emergency break

In reality, providers may have to take breaks from a few minutes to a few hours. The app can inform and re-arrange appointments and live queue participants so they can plan to wait in other places and return on time, or choose to reschedule themselves.
