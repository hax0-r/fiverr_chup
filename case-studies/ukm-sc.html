The Challenge

UKMSC operates as a specialist hospital, requiring patients to present a referral from their previous hospital or clinic before consulting with a doctor. Specialists have the discretion to accept or decline the referral. Once accepted, the hospital schedules the patient’s appointment.

The integration between different stages has been less effective, and communication between staff and patients has occasionally led to misunderstandings. These issues have sometimes disrupted the flow of operations

The hospital’s first-come, first-served system caused congestion during peak hours, with patients arriving early and waiting up to 4 hours. Doctor emergencies worsened delays, affects customer satisfaction

UKM Specialist Centre partnered with Chup Online to reduce patient wait-time uncertainty and enhance management efficiency

Pre-Approval Process

Chup Online's Pre-Approval Process lets patients upload referral letters before appointments. UKMSC staff review these in advance to assign patients to the right doctor, speeding up registration and reducing front desk congestion.

Auto-Reschedule Features

Chup Online's advanced automation improves patient flow with its Auto-rescheduling function, which adjusts appointments in real-time and keeps patients informed of delays. This dynamic scheduling reduces stress, boosts patient satisfaction, and helps healthcare providers maintain an efficient workflow, even during busy periods.

Fast Lane Button

Chup Online’s Fast Lane Button plays an essential role in the hospital’s queue management system, specifically designed to prioritize patients who need special attention, such as the elderly, individuals with disabilities (OKUs), and those in emergency situations. This feature ensures these patients bypass regular queues, allowing them to receive faster, tailored care that meets their specific needs. For the elderly and disabled, it reduces the strain of waiting for extended periods, while for emergencies, it provides immediate access to critical care, where timely intervention is vital.

Insightful Analytics Reports

Chup Online’s Analytics Report offers detailed, automatically updated insights into patient wait times and durations. Admins can export data for specific dates and times, including patient numbers, wait time summaries, and peak hour graphs. This powerful tool helps administrators allocate resources efficiently, optimize staff deployment, and improve the patient experience by identifying and addressing operational inefficiencies.

Automatic Identifier

When staff register or book a new appointment, our system uses the Automatic Identifier feature to streamline the process by retrieving existing patient data. This feature auto-fills essential details like WhatsApp numbers and IC numbers, accelerating registration and ensuring accuracy. This automation makes appointment booking more efficient and less time-consuming for both patients and staff, reducing manual data entry errors and allowing staff to focus on improving patient care and service quality.
