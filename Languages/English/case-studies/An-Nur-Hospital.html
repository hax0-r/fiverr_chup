The Challenge

An-Nur Hospital struggled with several patient flow issues that directly impacted their service efficiency. Long wait times were a significant concern, especially for special needs patients, as the manual processes for handling arrivals, registrations, and insurance verification created bottlenecks.

Staff were often overburdened, causing delays in processing patient details, verifying insurance, and managing real-time scheduling, which resulted in frustration for both staff and patients alike. Additionally, the lack of a cohesive system to handle early arrivals further contributed to an inefficient and disorganized patient journey, making it challenging for staff to provide the level of care that patients deserved.

With manual insurance verification in place, errors were frequent, leading to confusion regarding insurance status. Patients were left in the dark, waiting longer than necessary for approvals, while staff spent excessive time tracking and updating insurance records manually. Furthermore, patients arriving early for appointments were added to an already congested queue, causing frustration for both staff and patients.

Finally, pharmacy and billing processes suffered from inefficiencies, with patients waiting for medication and payment verification, disrupting their experience. Manual tracking caused delays in dispensing medication and collecting payments, worsening dissatisfaction and reducing operational efficiency.

The Solution

An-Nur Specialist Hospital partnered with Chup Online to elevate customer satisfaction and optimize hospital management. This collaboration focused on integrating advanced solutions to refine operational processes and enhance patient flow, while also developing customize features tailored to the hospital’s unique requirements, including:

Patients Journey Timeline

Tracks and displays the patient’s journey, including waiting and service times

Automatic Arrangement

Prioritizes and arranges patient appointments according to booked time slots

Transit Service

Guides patients through required procedures or services before seeing the doctor

Integrated Patients Token Number

Issues a consistent toke number fot patients throughout their visit

Insurance Verification & Billing Features

Customized features for HIAZ include additional buttons designed to streamline management during the Insurance verification and Billings states

Patient Journey Timeline

Chup Online's Patient Journey Timeline tracks each step of a patient’s visit, capturing details like waiting times and service durations to provide insights into care efficiency and enhance patient satisfaction.

At the end of the visit, the system provides a complete summary of the total visit duration, which can be easily shared or printed for record-keeping. This ensures accurate documentation and facilitates transparency and accountability in patient care. Both staff and patients can clearly understand the time spent during the visit.

Automatic Arrangement

The Automatic Arrange based on Appointment Time Feature manages both scheduled and walk-in patients by organizing them according to their time slots. It ensures a smooth flow by prioritizing appointments and handling each patient based on their arrival and schedule. This feature creates an efficient and orderly system for managing visits.

This integration streamlines daily operations for staff and enhances the patient experience by organizing all patients scheduled and walk-in into an orderly timeline. This reduces wait times and ensures a smooth, predictable visit, creating a more efficient and pleasant healthcare environment for everyone.

Transit Services

The Transit Service (Automatic Guide) feature enhances patient flow in healthcare facilities by providing automated, step-by-step guidance. Upon arrival, patients are directed to key service points, such as registration and lab tests, eliminating confusion and ensuring they know exactly where to go and what to do before their appointment.

By guiding patients through preliminary steps, the system reduces bottlenecks and waiting times, ensuring a smooth process. This enhances the patient experience and helps healthcare providers manage patient flow more effectively.

Integrated Patients Token Number

Chup streamlines the patient experience by assigning a consistent token number for the entire journey, eliminating the need for multiple numbers and reducing confusion across all departments and services.

This feature streamlines the patient journey and boosts operational efficiency by assigning a single token number for all services. This allows for seamless tracking and management, reducing wait times and errors. This uniform approach creates a more organized environment, enabling patients to focus on their healthcare needs without the hassle of managing multiple numbers.

Insurance Verification & Billing Button Features

Chup Online’s Verify Button and Edit Details features streamline insurance processing by enabling staff to update insurance status in real-time and customize patient profiles. This automation reduces manual errors and speeds up verification, ensuring patients receive timely updates on their insurance status.

Chup Online's "Mark As Paid" button and "Ready to Dispense" status automate patient movement between pharmacy and billing. This system allows real-time tracking and alerts for medication pickup, reducing waiting times and improving patient flow.

The Result

Chup Online transformed patient experience at An-Nur Hospital by offering special needs patients the Fast Lane, reducing wait times and improving access to care. By managing early arrivals efficiently, the hospital minimizes scheduling conflicts and enhances the patient environment. This streamlined approach reduces frustration, boosts staff productivity, and fosters compassion, ensuring timely care for all and meeting the diverse needs of the community.
